Serve the global community by curing all chronic diseases through a complete understanding of biological complexity at the molecular level.

Our Mission


Eliminate chronic diseases like Alzheimer’s and cancer through protein therapeutics.



Lead the worldwide protein structurome project to structure >80% of proteins in the human proteome.



Simulate robust clinical toxicity trials for the first time to exponentially accelerate drug R&D.

We will use protein structure information to develop protein therapeutics for chronic diseases such as Alzheimer's and cancer. This will make treatment discovery much more efficient and can save the lives of millions.

We envision possibilities beyond ourselves and for the future of scientific inquiry. Our time, and cost effective hand-held device can be used to catalog the structure of the human proteome which will then assist other scientists in their own biomedical work.

Current protein imaging techniques cost millions of dollars, require specially built labs to house the equipment, and are time intensive. Current clinical trial timelines require years to bring a novel drug to the patients. Protein structure is highly important in simulations and more accurate simulations will be able to speed up the process.
