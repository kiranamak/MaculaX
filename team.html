About Us

We value curiosity

We believe a supportive an innovative team culture is critical to a successful startup.

We encourage curiosity and thinking outside the box. We value people who are able to tackle a broad challenging problem and figure things out along the way especially when the path ahead is unclear. Since we operate on the edge of innovation, we need people able to dream big and try new things.

Failure is a necessary step towards success. We will need to repeatedly try and fail. This is an indication of our high standards for ourselves and our company because we will not settle for good enough.

We strive to build a meaningful and collaborative community of dedicated employees and most of all we hope to create a culture of learning. No one comes in knowing everything; there will be new things to discover and new challenges to overcome each day and through this, we will learn and grow for that is the process of life.
